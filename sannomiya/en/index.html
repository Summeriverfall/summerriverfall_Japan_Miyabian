About us

Wagyu buffet dining
in the heart of Kobe

Miyabian is Kobe Sannomiya's home of all-you-can-eat shabu-shabu and sukiyaki. Choose your favorite wagyu and enjoy it your way — shabu-shabu, sukiyaki, or our signature suki-shabu that lets you savor both at once. Every cut of meat is hand-sliced in-house, one piece at a time.

Each course also includes about 20 kinds of seasonal vegetables, udon, mochi, and an all-you-can-eat lineup of around 30 side dishes. The interior is brightened by chairs and noren from Kyoto textile brand SOUSOU, with private table rooms, tatami seating and couple seats. From family dinners to birthdays, parties and company banquets — the whole floor can be reserved for up to 80 guests.

We look forward to seeing you in Sannomiya.
