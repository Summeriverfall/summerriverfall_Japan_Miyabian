About us

Private room wagyu dining
on Tor Road

Set along Kobe's Tor Road, Miyabian Tor Road is the second location of Sannomiya's popular all-you-can-eat shabu-shabu and sukiyaki house Miyabian. Enjoy unlimited Kuroge Wagyu and world-famous Kobe Beef — as shabu-shabu, sukiyaki, or our signature suki-shabu.

Every table is a fully private room, seating from 2 up to 16 guests. With complete privacy, it suits business dinners, anniversaries and year-end parties alike — and families with children are always welcome. Courses also include about 20 kinds of vegetables and 30 all-you-can-eat side dishes, from obanzai to signature small plates.

A hideaway for an indulgent evening.
